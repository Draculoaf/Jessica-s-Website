Thoughts during and after website iteration four

The main changes that happened in the forth iteration of this site included: changing the design page into cards, and linking the cards to new pages which were designed to look the same as the blog posts. Having the same look helps to set up conventions for the users, but I also think that it makes the site look more uniform and professional.

The second major change of the site, is the addition of the slideshow on the home page. With this slideshow, I hope to make the viewer more enticed to click on the links in the timeline, and have them feel more interested in what I do overall.
